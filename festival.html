Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival

The Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival, is an International, competitive festival. Its mission is to provide a public forum in Brooklyn in order to advance public interest in films and the independent production of films. We aim to draw worldwide attention to Brooklyn as a center for cinema, to encourage the rights of all Brooklyn residents to access and experience the power of independent filmmaking, and to promote artistic excellence and the creative freedom of artists without censure.

Hosted on the 85-acre park on the Brooklyn side of the East River, the Brooklyn Outdoor Film festival is dedicated to movie lovers. Taking place on the Pier 3 Greenway Terrace, this is an an event where you can discover amazing films, meet people and it is the place to spent those warm nights on the begging of August.

Showtime starts every day from 6pm till midnight. The festival is free and open to audiences of all ages. RSVP not required, but encouraged.
